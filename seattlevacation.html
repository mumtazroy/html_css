Space Needle

I liked the Space Needle the best.

  • The Space Needle is an observation tower in Seattle, Washington
  • A landmark of the Pacific Northwest, and an icon of Seattle
  • Once the tallest structure west of the Mississippi River, it is 605 ft (184 m) high, 138 ft (42 m) wide, and weighs 9,550 tons
