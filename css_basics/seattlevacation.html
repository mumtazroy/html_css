The Seattle Skyline...

A view from Queen Anne Hill. The Space Needle is visible on the left

The mountain in the background is Mount Rainier, on the right is Elliott Bay and the Port of Seattle on Puget Sound.

However, 16 high rise projects will change the skyline in the future.

Space Needle

I liked the Space Needle the best.

  • The Space Needle is an observation tower in Seattle, Washington
  • A landmark of the Pacific Northwest, and an icon of Seattle
  • Once the tallest structure west of the Mississippi River, it is 605 ft (184 m) high, 138 ft (42 m) wide, and weighs 9,550 tons
